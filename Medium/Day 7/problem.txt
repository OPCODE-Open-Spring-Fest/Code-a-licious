Problem Statment

Bob is going to use n lamps and k different colours of paint to make a garland to adorn a New Year's tree. But he has a regulation that says you can't put two lamps of the same colour next to each other. How many different ways can he arrange the garland?

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Input
Contain two integer n,k - no. of lamps & no. of different paints (1<=k,n<=15)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Output
Print the number of painting ways. If Bob can not paint a
garland after the described requirements, to show out -1.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Time limit 1 seconds
Memory limit 64 megabytes
